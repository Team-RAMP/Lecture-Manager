Manage & Track your Attendance with Ease


Timely is a one-stop personalized solution for easily keeping tracking of your lectures

Features

Visualise & Track Attendance

Visualise your timetables to track your attendance and lecture progress.

Manage Attendance

Add your attendance and view your attendance percentages for each subject.

Timely Notifications

Get notifications that remind you to log your attendance. Dont like it? Turn it off right away!

Privacy Focused Data Storage

We don't store your data on our servers without your consent and it remains locally on your device.

Store Lecture Notes

Add text, photo, audio, video, special formatting, etc as your notes for your lectures.

Offline availability

Don't have an internet connection? Not an issue. Timely will always be there for you!
